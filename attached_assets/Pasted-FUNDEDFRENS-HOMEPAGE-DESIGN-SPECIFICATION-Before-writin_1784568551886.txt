FUNDEDFRENS — HOMEPAGE DESIGN SPECIFICATION

Before writing or changing any UI, understand what FundedFrens is.

FundedFrens is not a traditional forex prop firm.

It is a crypto prop trading platform built specifically for Solana meme coin traders.

Users prove they can trade profitably during a Demo Challenge.

Once they pass and are approved, they unlock a funded trading account.

The platform combines:
