FUNDEDFRENS — PREMIUM DASHBOARD SPECIFICATION

The dashboard is the heart of FundedFrens.

This is where users spend almost all of their time.

The dashboard should feel comparable to a premium fintech platform or professional trading terminal—not because it is crowded, but because information is organized clearly and intelligently.

Every section should have a clear purpose.

Every statistic should have a home.

The user should never wonder where to find something.

The UI should be clean, premium, responsive, and easy to understand.

⸻

DASHBOARD LAYOUT

The dashboard should be divided into independent sections.

Do NOT place everything inside one large page.

FINAL GOAL

The dashboard should immediately make users feel they are using a premium crypto prop trading platform.

It should present complex trading information in a way that feels organized, approachable, and professional.

The user should always know:

* Where they are in the challenge.
* How they’re performing.
* Whether they’re respecting the rules.
* What they need to do next.
* How close they are to unlocking a funded account.

The experience should inspire confidence, clarity, and trust while remaining visually refined and easy to navigate.
